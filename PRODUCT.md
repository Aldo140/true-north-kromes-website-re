# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Canadian dental laboratories, denturists, and dental professionals evaluating a production partner for cobalt-chrome partial denture frameworks. They need to understand the workflow, verify technical credibility, inspect finished work, and start a case quickly.

## Product Purpose

Present True North Kromes as a complete Canadian digital manufacturing lab for CAD design, SLM metal printing, plasma polishing, inspection, and delivery. Success means visitors immediately understand the service, trust the production process, and contact the lab or enter the client portal.

## Brand Personality

Precise, mechanical, and assured. The experience should feel like observing a controlled production line rather than browsing a generic healthcare brochure.

## Anti-references

- Generic dental or medical templates with stock-white clinical styling
- SaaS card grids, decorative gradients, and interchangeable landing-page layouts
- Direct imitation of another company's copy or exact composition
- Motion added without production meaning, especially effects that obscure content

## Design Principles

- Show the actual process and actual finished work.
- Make technical detail understandable without flattening it into marketing claims.
- Use motion to explain sequence, handoff, registration, and state.
- Keep the gold accent scarce and functional, like a registration mark.
- Preserve direct routes to contact, directions, and the client portal.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard access. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`, preserve readable static alternatives for every animated section, avoid scroll hijacking on small screens, and provide pause controls for continuous informational motion.
